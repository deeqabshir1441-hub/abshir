Welcome to TV96 Live

Welcome to TV96 Live, your trusted destination for football news, live match updates, fixtures, league standings and comprehensive sports coverage from around the world.

Football is more than just a sport—it is a passion shared by millions of people every day. Our mission is to keep football fans connected through accurate information, useful articles, breaking news, and regular match updates.

Whether you follow club football, international tournaments, or your favorite national team, TV96 Live is designed to provide reliable sports content in a clean, fast and easy-to-use platform.

Our goal is to become one of the most trusted independent football websites for sports fans around the world.

Who We Are

TV96 Live is an independent football website created to deliver high-quality sports information to football supporters everywhere.

Our platform combines football news, match previews, league standings, fixtures, transfer stories, player updates, and football analysis into one place.

Unlike websites that only display scores, we aim to provide useful football content that helps visitors better understand the game.

Every improvement we make focuses on creating a better experience for our readers while continuously expanding our football coverage across different competitions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide football supporters with reliable, original, accurate and easy-to-understand sports information.

  • Publish quality football articles.
  • Deliver fast football updates.
  • Cover major football competitions.
  • Create original sports content.
  • Provide an enjoyable reading experience.
  • Continuously improve our platform.

We believe every football fan deserves access to trustworthy football news without unnecessary complexity.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to become a respected global football platform that serves fans from every continent.

As football continues to evolve, TV96 Live will continue expanding its coverage, introducing new features, publishing original analysis and providing valuable sports information every day.

Quality, accuracy and transparency remain at the center of everything we publish.

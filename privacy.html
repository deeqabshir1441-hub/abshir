Introduction

The Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit and purchase a product from the Site (TV96 Live).

Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device. This information includes your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some cookies on your device. In addition, as you browse the site, we collect information about the pages or products that you view, what keywords or sites you used to refer you to the site, and information about how you interact with the site. This information is called "Device Information".

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • Cookies: These are files that are placed on your computer and usually consist of a unique identifier. For more information about our cookies, please visit Cookie Policy. For more information about cookies and to disable them, visit your browser settings.
  • Log Files: They track events on the site and include IP information, browser type, Internet service provider, the pages the visitor entered and exited from, and the times and dates.
  • Web Beacons: Certain pages on our Sites contain web beacons (also known as Internet tags, pixel tags, clear GIFs, or JavaScript equivalents). These web beacons allow third parties to obtain information such as the IP address of the computer that loaded the page on which the beacon appears, the URL of the page on which the beacon appears, the time the page containing the beacon was viewed, and the type of browser used to display the page, and information contained in cookies set by third parties.

How We Use Personal Information

We use the information to carry out any order made through the site, and this ensures that payments are processed, shipping, invoices are provided, or payments are confirmed. In addition, we use this information to:

  • Communicate with you regarding your orders and account
  • Monitor orders to avoid any fraud or unauthorized transactions
  • Display advertisements related to our products or services based on your preferences
  • Monitor operations to reduce the risk of fraud, especially with regard to IP addresses
  • Develop and organize the site by analyzing customer statistics and interaction patterns
  • Organize more successful advertising campaigns and marketing initiatives

Note: We only use your information for legitimate business purposes and to improve your experience on our platform.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your personal information with third parties to help us benefit from it, as described above. We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use our site. You can learn more about how information from Google Analytics is used at: Google Privacy Policy.

You can also request that your data be not tracked from Google Analytics via the following link: Google Analytics Opt-out.

Finally, we may share your personal information to comply with special laws and regulations, or subpoenas or searches, or any other legal process or requests for information.

Behavioral Advertising

As described above, we use your personal information to provide you with targeted advertisements that we think may be of interest to you. For more information on how targeted advertising works, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative's ("NAI") educational page at: NAI Educational Page.

You can opt out of targeted ads via the following links:

  • Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Google Ad Settings
  • Bing Ads Personalization

Additionally, you may opt out of some of these services by visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out portal at: About Ads Opt-out.

Do Not Track

Please note that we do not tamper or modify the data collected when you see the "Do Not Track" tag in your browser.

Your Rights

If you reside in a European country, you have the right to access your personal information that we collect about you and to request an update, addition, or deletion of your information. You can exercise your right by contacting us via the information below.

In addition, if you are a resident of European countries, we would like to inform you that we are processing your information to fulfill the requirements of agreements we may have with you (for example, if you place an order through the site) or to fulfill the business requirements that were previously explained.

Important: Please note that your information will be transferred outside Europe, including Canada and the United States.

Data Retention

When you place an order through the Site, we will secure your order information for our records unless you request removal of that information. You may contact us at any time to request deletion of your personal data.

Minors

The site is not intended for persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect any changes in practices or processes or for legal purposes. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
